A London Made Honey

Regent's Park Honey is produced entirely in the beautiful London park. The bees forage from the abundance of trees and flowers that are found in the beautiful 166 hectare area spanning the London boroughs of Camden and Westminster.

A Unique & Natural Taste

The honey is spun off the honeycomb and gravity filtered. After settling it is jarred. The raw honey the bees produce has a rich, delicate flavour with a slight citrus note. A classic London honey.
